Orchard & Meadow

A serene and ecologically rich landscape feature, the Orchard & Meadow area blends productivity with natural beauty. Rows of apple trees are interplanted with a curated Northeast wildflower mix, designed to enhance seasonal color, pollinator habitat, and biodiversity.
Gently mown grass paths weave through the space, inviting strolling, maintenance access, and quiet immersion in the landscape. This area serves as a transitional, sensory-rich zone connecting more structured spaces with the wider natural site context.

Kitchen / Potager Garden

This productive garden space is anchored by raised beds for vegetables, herbs, and cut flowers, arranged with classic symmetry and functionality. A seasonal greenhouse and potting shed provide space for seed starting and tool storage, extending the growing season and enhancing horticultural flexibility.
Pea-stone paths offer crisp lines and comfortable footing between beds, tying the space together with a clean, timeless material palette. Designed for both utility and charm, the potager garden supports a vibrant, seasonal rhythm of growing and harvesting.

Fire Pit

Set beyond the pool as a destination and evening focal point, the reclaimed granite fire pit adds rustic character and permanence. The pit's generous proportions surround a 300,000 BTU burner, designed to produce a realistic, campfire-like flame with ample warmth for extended outdoor use. Encircled by informal seating, this area provides a natural gathering place for conversation and quiet enjoyment after sunset.

Cabana Kitchen

An informal, welcoming outdoor cooking and gathering area is framed by long planks of bluestone, which appear to dissolve organically into surrounding perennial gardens. The softened hardscape edge creates a sense of movement and impermanence, blurring the line between structure and nature. Ideal for both everyday outdoor meals and larger gatherings, the space accommodates flexible tables and seating arrangements, offering a relaxed, convivial setting for entertaining.

Pool & Lounge Area

A 20' x 40' swimming pool anchors this sleek and understated lawn area, with 30" wide bluestone coping lending a refined, architectural edge. The design emphasizes simplicity and proportion, allowing the pool to feel integrated and serene within the broader landscape. The adjacent lounge area features generous seating capacity, shaded by a striking dual cantilever Tuuci umbrella, providing comfort, elegance, and visual balance for sunbathing and casual relaxation.
